# Independent verification (baseline rule for every role)

Trust nothing from prior turns — verify every claim by reading actual files and
running commands yourself. Do not assume a planner's design, an executor's
self-report, or a prior reviewer's verdict is correct.

`THREAD.md` is a turn log (context), not evidence. `GOAL.md` and the repository
are the only ground truth; `THREAD_SUMMARY.md` is a lossy compaction and may
have stripped the evidence chain.
